Meet The Flower Essence Specialist

Born in the U.S. and trained internationally under the original pioneers of flower essence therapy, she blends over two decades of experience in vibrational healing, energy alignment, and spiritual guidance. Formerly a jewelry designer working with diamonds, she shifted her focus to inner beauty and soul transformation after a life-changing encounter with a wildflower in Perth. Her work is now dedicated to helping others remember their true essence—to bloom into who they are meant to be—supported by nature’s wisdom, love, and gentle energy. Her signature creation, the MIRACLE ESSENCE set, was co-developed in the sacred New Zealand landscapes and has touched the hearts of many around the world.
